Who We Are

IIITB COMET Foundation is a Section 8 company and a Technology Innovation Hub (TIH) in the areas of Advanced Communication Systems. We were established by IIIT Bangalore under the National Mission on Interdisciplinary Cyber-Physical Systems (NM-ICPS) of the Department of Science and Technology (DST), Government of India.

Our mandate is to bridge the gap between academic research and industrial deployment, fostering world-class technology development and skill enhancement in 5G and beyond wireless domains.

Program Structure

Three comprehensive modules covering the full spectrum of wireless communications

Module 1

Digital Design & Embedded Programming

2 Months 45,000

Covers digital design, programming (assembly/Embedded C), IoT, and Wired Protocols using Arduino, Raspberry Pi, and Pico W.

  • Digital Design Fundamentals
  • Embedded C Programming
  • Python AI Programming
  • Wired Protocols (UART/SPI/I2C)
Module 2
Most Popular

Design of 5G Networks with Hands-on

4 Months 75,000

Comprehensive coverage of 5G architecture, protocols, and implementation with practical hands-on experience.

  • Evolution from 1G to 5G
  • 5G Core Architecture
  • RAN L2/L3 Protocols
  • Physical Layer Implementation
Module 3 (Optional)

AI/ML for 5G and 6G Wireless

3 Months 75,000

Advanced AI/ML techniques for next-generation wireless communications with Python implementations.

  • Machine Learning Fundamentals
  • Wireless Communication with Python
  • Massive MIMO Design
  • Modulation Classification
